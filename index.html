Let's Ping Some Pongs!

Please enter a number, and we'll apply the rules of Ping Pong! That is, we'll give you all the numbers from 1 to your number, and apply these rules:

------------------

Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "ping"

Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong"

Numbers divisible by 15 are replaced with "pingpong"

------------------

Got that? Time to play!
