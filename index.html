Let's Ping Some Pongs!

Enter a number, and we'll apply the rules of Ping Pong! That is, we'll give you all the numbers from 1 to your number, and apply these rules:

------------------

Numbers divisible by 3 are replaced with "Ping"

Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "Pong"

Numbers divisible by 3 AND 5 are replaced with "Ping-Pong!"

------------------

Got that? Time to play!
